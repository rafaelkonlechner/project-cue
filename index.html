Join the community!

Project Cue is a language agnostic and collaborative question & answer platform which tries to bring together people who need help and people who want to provide help. All in everybodies native language!

Become a very early member by scanning the provided QR code with your mobile phone, or by manually typing in the url into your address bar (currently, the app works for mobile phones only).

Collaboration

Earn reputation by asking questions or answering them or simply rate existing ones to help bringing forward the most helpful content. Verify your profile if you work for an aid agency to increase your credibility even further.

Seamless translation

Everything is provided in your own native language. The translation happens in the background, so you do not need to worry about it. If you happen to be bilinguar, help us improve on translations where necessary.

Context awareness

The ranking of answers is not solely based on rankings, but rather depends on a multitude of other factors such as geographical proximity (where adequate), up-to-dateness as well as the credibility of users.
